Okan Efe Öğretmen

Soon-to-be Ph.D. (Aug 2026) & XR Designer-Researcher

Koç University · KARMA Mixed Reality Lab · Sarıyer, İstanbul

I'm a multidisciplinary researcher, designer, and developer of consumer and enterprise XR experiences — a fast learner, creative, and passionate about visual design, ambitious to predict and shape the future by creating. My Ph.D. work explores XR-mediated communication in domestic spaces, spanning co-design studies, participant observation, and Unity/C# prototyping, evaluated through thematic analysis and the Continuous Fuzzy Kano Model.

I'm graduating with my Ph.D. in August and actively looking for an assistant professor position. I'm also up for jumping into projects as an XR & full-stack developer, and I'd love to team up with a lab as a visiting researcher — thinking about UX research through XR, weaving XR into product design, bringing it into everyday spaces like the kitchen, or designing UI for XR.
